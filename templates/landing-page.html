@Kozminski_AI

We are the trailblazers of Kozminski University, the first-ever tech student club created in November 2022 by a group of young professionals with diverse backgrounds in tech and business. Our passion is to nurture curious minds and help them develop their technical skills through real-life projects, educational materials, and exchanges with like-minded individuals. But that's not all, we also believe in giving back to the tech industry by hosting networking events, inviting guest lecturers, and providing tech support for founders with innovative startup ideas.

We are always on the lookout for opportunities to collaborate with startups and support the development of new technology. If you have a business idea that could use our expertise and support, we're all ears! Get in touch to learn more about how we can work together to bring your vision to life.

We're constantly striving to bring in experienced tech professionals as guest speakers for our lectures and events. If you're a tech guru with a wealth of knowledge and experience and are interested in sharing your insights with young tech professionals, we welcome you to join us as a speaker.

Follow us on LinkedIn to stay updated on our upcoming events and projects, and join us in our mission to learn, grow, and make a positive impact through technology.
