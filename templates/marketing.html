Marketing and AI

Our club hosted a remarkable event at Kozminski University, featuring Piotr Bombol, a marketing maestro who shared his secrets on creating AI-powered marketing tools without writing a single line of code. The knowledge gained from this invaluable experience is nothing short of practical gold for students looking to make waves in the industry.

But the event did not stop there! The conference attendees took things to the next level by collaborating on a movie recommendation tool. Using Zapier, ChatGPT-3, and email, they developed a tool that considers your mood, feelings, and current situation to provide a movie suggestion that will knock your socks off. Move over Netflix, there's a new player in town! We can't wait to see what incredible things we'll achieve and learn at our next event!
